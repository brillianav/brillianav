## About Me

I started my career as a web developer in 2014, focusing on frontend development and WordPress. Since then, I have been building websites for businesses that need something efficient, budget-friendly, and easy to manage after launch.

- I work mainly with WordPress, custom themes, plugins, frontend development, and website maintenance.
- I care about websites that are fast, clear, responsive, and simple for real users to operate.
- I enjoy helping businesses translate messy ideas into useful digital experiences.
- When I am not coding, I am probably watching football. I will still reply, promise.
